Pinellas County Advanced Academic Curriculum Programs

Pinellas County offers two advanced academic curriculum and assessment high school programs - Cambridge (AICE) and International Baccalaureate (IB). To attend these programs, parents must submit an online application during the Application Period to get on the waitlist. The Application Period usually takes place in January.
